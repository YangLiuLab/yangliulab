Welcome to the laboratory of Dr. Yang Liu at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. We are pioneering the future of precision medicine through cutting-edge multiscale optical microscopy, automation and robotics, artificial intelligence, and large-scale bioimage informatics. Our imaging techniques span seven orders of magnitude—from the nanoscale to the mesoscale—enabling transformative advancements in precision medicine. Our lab's fusion of cross-scale imaging and AI-driven systems biology is setting the stage for unprecedented scientific discoveries and transformative personalized medicine.
