Graduate Students

Yaxin Yang

Yaxin Yang is a graduate student in the Department of Bioengineering at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Her current research focuses on genomic and large-scale image analysis to identify the molecular and physical traits of cancer cells when they develop resistance to chemotherapy.

Chaojie Zhang

Chaojie Zhang is a graduate student in the Department of Bioengineering at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. His current research focuses on single-cell morphodynamics to predict the cell response to theraupetics.

Dimitrios Gotsis

Dimitrios is a graduate student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. His current research focuses on large-scale image analysis of spatial omics data in inflammatory bowel diseases.

Shuyuan Zhang

Shuyuan is a graduate student in the Department of Bioengineering at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Her current research focuses on biological investigation of epigenetic factors and tumor microenvironment in drug resistance of cancer cells.

Shengjie Ma

Shengjie is a master student in the Department of Computer Science at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. He is working on the development of software package for super-resolution image reconstruction and image analysis.

Vidhi Parmar

Vidhi is a master student in the Department of Bioengineering at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. She is developing low-cost long-term live-cell imaging devices.

Shengwei Chen

Shengwei is a Mechanical Engineering student at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. He is developing automated imaging and sample preparation devices.
