Open Positions

We are looking for talented and highly motivated individual to work on development and application of high-throughput super-resolution imaging techniques (primarily single molecule localization microscopy) to improve cancer detection. If your background is in biophysics research and single molecule imaging, this position will allow you to explore the application of the super-resolution imaging techniques to solve real-world challenges in cancer research. If you are an engineer with a background in optical imaging, this position will allow you to develop new capabilities of super-resolution microscopy and single molecule analysis to address unmet clinical need. If you are an electrical engineer with an expertise in image processing, this position will allow you to develop image analysis tools to improve the throughput and automated analysis of large dataset of super-resolution microscopy images for clinical use.
